Who we are

Parashar Jyotisha is a spiritual and educational Vedic astrology platform created to make traditional Jyotisha, Panchanga, Kundli analysis, transit awareness and practical spiritual guidance easier to understand for modern users.

Our connected products include the Vedic Panchanga App, the Parashar Jyotisha / Jyotisha App, and this website.

What we offer

  • Daily Panchanga, Tithi, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana and Muhurta information.

  • Kundli matching / compatibility tools.
  • Numerology reports and compatibility insights.
