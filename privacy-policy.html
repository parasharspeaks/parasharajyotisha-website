Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Parashar Jyotisha website, Vedic Panchanga App, Parashar Jyotisha App, consultations, reports and payment services.

Last updated: 14 May 2026Website: https://parasharajyotisha.comApps: Vedic Panchanga App & Parashar Jyotisha App

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to Parashar Jyotisha, our website, the Vedic Panchanga App, the Parashar Jyotisha / Jyotisha App, APIs, digital reports, consultations and payment services.

Information we may collect

  • Birth and astrology data: name, gender if provided, date of birth, time of birth, place/city of birth, latitude/longitude derived from city, relationship/profile labels and saved profiles.
  • Panchanga and location data: city, country, manually selected location, approximate or precise location if you grant location permission.
  • Account data: email address, phone number, login identifiers and authentication information if account login is enabled.
  • Consultation data: questions, booking details, contact details, birth details, messages and documents/images voluntarily provided.
  • Payment data: order ID, payment status, payment amount and transaction references. Card, UPI, net-banking and wallet details are processed by gateways such as Razorpay and are not stored by us in full.
  • Device and app data: app version, device model, operating system, crash logs, diagnostics, usage events, push notification token and technical logs.
  • Support data: email messages, feedback, complaint details and screenshots voluntarily shared with us.

How we use information

  • To calculate Panchanga, Kundli, Dasha, Yogas, Doshas, matching, transit, numerology and related outputs.
  • To provide consultations, digital reports, remedies and customer support.
  • To process payments, refunds and invoices.
  • To save preferences such as city, language, profile and notification settings.
  • To send reminders or notifications if enabled by the user.
  • To improve app performance, diagnose errors and protect services from misuse.
  • To comply with legal, tax, accounting, payment gateway and platform requirements.

Sharing of information

We do not sell user personal data. We may share limited information with trusted service providers only as needed to operate our services, such as cloud/API hosting providers, Firebase/Google services, payment gateways such as Razorpay, communication tools, and legal/regulatory authorities when required.

Payments

Payments may be processed by Razorpay or another payment gateway. We receive payment confirmation, order identifiers and transaction status, but we do not store full card numbers, UPI PINs, net-banking passwords or wallet credentials.

Data retention and deletion

We retain data only as long as reasonably required for app functionality, consultation records, legal compliance, tax/accounting requirements, fraud prevention and dispute handling. Users may request deletion through our Data Deletion page.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards such as HTTPS where available, platform security features and restricted access. No internet transmission or electronic storage system is 100% secure.

Children and minors

Our astrology and Panchanga apps are not intended for unsupervised use by children. Users under 18 should use the services only with parental or guardian guidance, especially for consultations or payments.
