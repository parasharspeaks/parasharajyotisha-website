Data Deletion Request

Data Deletion page for Google Play Console and users of Vedic Panchanga App, Parashar Jyotisha App and website services.

Request deletion of your account or app data

If you use our apps or website and want to delete your account or associated personal data, you can request deletion by email.

Email request

Send an email to silenceofdark29@gmail.com with subject line Data Deletion Request - Parashar Jyotisha.

Please include your name, registered email/phone, app name and what data you want deleted.

In-app deletion path

If account login is enabled in the app, use: App → Profile / Settings → Account → Delete Account.

If this option is not yet visible, use the email deletion request above. Before Google Play submission, account-creation apps should include an in-app deletion path.

What may be deleted

  • Account profile data
  • Saved birth profiles and preferences
  • Saved city/favorite/recent location preferences
  • Consultation request data where legally permitted
  • Notification tokens and app identifiers linked to your account

Data we may retain

Some records may be retained where required for legal, tax, accounting, fraud prevention, security, payment dispute, chargeback or regulatory reasons.

Timeline

We aim to acknowledge deletion requests within 7 business days and complete eligible deletion within a reasonable time, subject to identity verification and legal obligations.
