Refund & Cancellation Policy

Refund and Cancellation Policy for Parashar Jyotisha website, apps, consultation bookings, digital astrology reports and subscriptions.

Last updated: 14 May 2026Website: https://parasharajyotisha.comApps: Vedic Panchanga App & Parashar Jyotisha App

General principle

We aim to be fair and transparent. Because many of our services are digital, personalized and time-based, refund eligibility depends on the type and stage of service.

Consultation cancellation

  • More than 24 hours before scheduled consultation: user may request rescheduling or cancellation.
  • Less than 24 hours before scheduled consultation: cancellation may not be eligible for full refund, but rescheduling may be considered case by case.
  • No-show by user: no refund is normally provided.
  • Cancellation by us: full refund or rescheduling will be offered.

Digital reports

  • Once a personalized Kundli, Panchanga, matching, numerology or astrology report is generated, delivered or made available for download, it is normally non-refundable.
  • If the report is not generated due to a technical error and we cannot deliver it within a reasonable time, a refund or fresh report may be offered.
  • Refund is not available for incorrect birth details entered by the user after report generation.

Duplicate payment / failed transaction

  • Duplicate successful payments for the same order will be reviewed and eligible duplicate amounts will be refunded.
  • If payment is debited but order is not confirmed, please contact us with transaction details.

Refund timeline

Approved refunds will be initiated to the original payment method within 5–7 business days after approval. Actual credit time may depend on the bank, card network, UPI provider, wallet or payment gateway.

How to request cancellation or refund

Email silenceofdark29@gmail.com with full name, registered email/phone, order ID, Razorpay payment ID if available, payment date, amount and reason.

Not normally eligible

  • Dissatisfaction with interpretive astrology outcome after report delivery.
  • Change of mind after digital report generation.
  • User-provided wrong birth details after report generation.
  • No-show for confirmed consultation without prior communication.
